About Us

Swell Software Solutions

Swell Software Solutions is a dynamic team of six enthusiastic students from the University of Victoria (UVic). We specialize in designing and developing user-friendly applications tailored to solve real-world problems. With a shared passion for technology and innovation, we combine our diverse skill sets in software engineering, UX design, and project management to create impactful digital solutions. Our collaborative spirit drives us to tackle complex challenges, pushing each other to deliver quality software that meets the needs of our users. As UVic students, we are keenly aware of the challenges faced by fellow students, and our current project aims to address these through thoughtful design and seamless functionality.

The Client

University of Victoria

The University of Victoria (UVic) is a leading research-intensive institution located in Victoria, British Columbia. Known for its picturesque campus, UVic offers a blend of rigorous academic programs and a vibrant student community. With a focus on experiential learning, research, and sustainability, UVic provides a supportive environment for over 22,000 students across various disciplines. Its commitment to excellence is reflected in its innovative programs, cutting-edge research facilities, and a wide range of extracurricular opportunities. UVic’s welcoming atmosphere, beautiful surroundings, and strong industry connections make it a hub for student growth and professional development.

Our Project

RingMap

Our project is an innovative campus navigation app designed to help students easily locate amenities within university buildings. As UVic students ourselves, we know how challenging it can be to find essential facilities, such as study spaces, accessible washrooms, or departmental offices, especially in larger or newly renovated buildings. Our app provides detailed floor plans and highlights key amenities, enabling students to navigate the campus more confidently. By making information readily available and accessible, we aim to enhance the UVic experience, helping students save time and reduce frustration as they explore the university’s diverse spaces.
